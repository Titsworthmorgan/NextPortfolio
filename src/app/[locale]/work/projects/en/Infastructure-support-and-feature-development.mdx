## Overview

Fresh Country Fund Raising offers schools and organizations the opportunity to raise money through its professionally developed Fund Raising Programs. The platform provides a variety of products, including gift wrap, cookie dough, and other popular items, allowing groups to choose the best fit for their fundraising needs. With a focus on quality products and exceptional customer service, Fresh Country has become a trusted partner for countless organizations looking to achieve their fundraising goals.

## Key Features

- **Infrastructure Support**: Provided ongoing maintenance and support for the existing platform to ensure stability and reliability.
- **Feature Development**: Implemented new features to enhance user experience and operational efficiency.
- **Bug Fixes**: Addressed and resolved various bugs to ensure smooth functionality.
- **Performance Optimization**: Improved the platform's performance for better responsiveness and user satisfaction.
- **User Interface Enhancements**: Refined the UI to make it more intuitive and user-friendly.

## Technologies Used

- **MeteorJS**: For building real-time web applications.
- **MongoDB**: For flexible and scalable database management.
- **Docker VMs**: For containerized development and deployment environments.

## Challenges and Learnings

The project presented several challenges, including maintaining and enhancing an existing platform while ensuring minimal disruption to users. Updating outdated/legacy code and integrating new features required careful planning and execution. Through collaboration we were able to prioritize tasks effectively and deliver high-quality deliverables. This experience reinforced the importance of clear communication, agile development practices, and continuous learning in a fast-paced environment.

## Outcome

The Fresh Country platform was successfully maintained and enhanced, providing a more robust solution for fundraising organizations. The development team not only gained valuable experience in infrastructure support and feature development but also contributed to a meaningful project that positively impacts the lives of many communities.
